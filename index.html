We build tools that help brand managers, and researchers discover, analyze, and monitor domain names across their full lifecycle. Our open-source libraries power fast word segmentation for domain names, and our MCP server brings domain intelligence directly into AI workflows through Claude, Cursor, and other MCP-compatible clients.
